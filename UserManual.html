This application is a simple phonebook which stores data locally.

 

Welcome page

This is the welcome page, to change themes, simply touch dark theme or light theme.

To get to the camera page, touch the camera button.

To navigate to the phonebook, swipe left on the screen.

 

List page

This is the list page. The contacts inside of the list will be sorted alphabetically. To reverse the sorting simply press the Z-A button. If you want to set the sorting back to alphabetical you can press the A-Z button.

To add a new contact press the + button.

To delete a contact, touch and hold your finger on a contact and then drag their name to the bin up on the top right of the screen.

To see a contacts full details, just tap their name.

 

Add page

This is the add page.

Simply input the contacts information and press add and they will be added to the database/contact list.

Edit page

To edit a contacts information you will need to slide their name to the left and then press the red pencil that shows up.

Once on the page of the contact you wish to edit, change whatever details you want to change and press the update button.

 

Detail page

This is the detail page. It will show you the full details of a contact once you have tapped them.

Camera page

This is the camera page. To exit press the back button and to take a picture press the small camera button.

After you take a picture you will see this screen and can choose whether to save the picture, delete the picture or discard it and try again.
